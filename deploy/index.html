The NOAA Fisheries Integrated Toolbox (NOAA FIT) is an interdisciplinary, web-based portal of tools used for a variety of applications, including stock assessment modeling, data preparation for fish and protected species, and economic and ecosystem modeling. This web portal facilitates easier sharing and comparison of analytical tools. The NOAA FIT includes tools developed by NOAA scientists and programmers, as well as those developed in collaboration with, and exclusively by external developers. The NOAA FIT also provides resources for developers and users of the tools to ensure best practices and ease of use.The NOAA FIT is maintained by the National Modeling Team in NOAA Fisheries Office of Science and Technology.

Contact the toolbox team for more information on tool development and usage.
